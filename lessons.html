What We Teach

Beginner Lessons

As a beginner we start at the beginning. We intorduce the fun of riding in a safe way. How to tack-up properly and what everything is called.

All Ages

Our classes are for all ages. We make sure that your child is safe.They are taught the correct way to lead a horse.

Correct Foundations

We teach children how to mount correctly. How to start and stop by using their reins and their feet and how to balance properly on the horse.

Comradery

The most important of all lessons is to have fun. Make new friends and enjoy your time here with us.

A Quick Lesson

In the video below you will see what we call a Lunge Lesson.
This is where the instructor has the pony on a lunge rein and the rider is learning to control the pony.
This is the safest way to rider until the rider has confidence and skill enough to ride alone.
A young rider will learn how to trot and canter in a safe and controlled manner.
