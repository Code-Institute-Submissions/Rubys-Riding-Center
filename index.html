What We Encourage

Building Trust

When you spend time with a horse, brushing and talkingto him and sneaking the odd carrot, you build a bond with him and learn to trust him.

Confidence

All children want to love and be loved and children with disabilitis are no different. They grow in confidence and thrive in a horses company.

Learning to Ride

Lessons are given by a qualified instructor to groups or one on one. The students are given the opertunity to practise what they have learnt.

Having Fun

Enjoying new found freedom. New experiences with your horse as you ride in the sunshine through nature.

Group Outrides

These are specially arranged outings which are normally on the first Saturday of each month.

Compertitions

We have class compertitions where the riders get to show off their new found talents.
